---------------------FILE SYSTEMS-----------
method of organizing pieces of data on a disk

commonly used 
NTFS
-default on Windows
-supports many advanced features ( journaling , compression and encryption etc)

FAT family
-default in older Windows and MS-DOS
-commonly used on flash drives and embedded devices
-easy to learn 

APFS
-default in Mac OS X 
-built to address the many limitations in previously used HFS+
-has advanced features(snapshots, encryption,compression,crash protection etc)

ext family 
most commonly used on linux systems
ext3 introduced journaling
